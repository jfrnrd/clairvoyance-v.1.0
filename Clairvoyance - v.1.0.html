02 / Protocol Layer

How Markets Actually Work

The mechanics that make combinatorial pricing honest, scalable, and resistant to manipulation.

Correlation-Aware AMM
Protocol · Pricing Engine
Dynamic Correlation Matrix
Prices combos using historical co-movement data, not naive probability multiplication. BTC + ETH together is priced with their real 0.88 correlation factored in.
Anti-Manipulation Oracle Mesh New
Outcomes resolved by a weighted mesh of on-chain oracles, social consensus signals, and institutional data feeds — not a single point of failure.
Partial Fill Engine New
If a 7-leg combo has only 4 legs with deep liquidity, you still get filled at fair odds on those 4. The other 3 queue. No all-or-nothing execution failures.
Dynamic Liquidity Pools
LPs earn tiered rewards calibrated to combo complexity. Simple 2-leg combos earn base rate. 8-leg combos earn 6×. Complexity is compensated, not penalized.
Position Primitives
Protocol · Financial Instruments
Composable Position NFTs
Every combo bet mints an ERC-721. Trade it, collateralize it in DeFi, gift it, or split it into pieces. Your position has life beyond the original bet.
Leg Extraction New
Sell individual legs out of your combo as the market moves. Won 3 of 5 events? Strip those wins into liquid shares. Keep betting on the remaining 2 independently.
Structured Outcome Tokens New
Convert a resolved winning position into a fixed-maturity structured token. Earn yield on prediction winnings while waiting for final settlement.
Progressive Payout
As individual legs resolve, partial payouts unlock proportionally. No waiting 6 months to collect anything on already-correct earlier calls.

05 / What's Actually New

Features Polymarket & Kalshi Can't Match

Not incremental improvements — structural advantages that require rethinking what a prediction market is.

Thesis Canvas New

Type "I think AI regulation will pass before the next election and crush Nvidia's margins." The system maps this to real markets. You think in English; Clairvoyance translates to bets.

Cross-Platform Arbitrage Scanner New

Continuously compares combo-implied probabilities against Polymarket and Kalshi's simple market prices. Surface genuine mispricings before they close. Polymarket and Kalshi are blind to each other — you won't be.

Flash Combos — 24hr Markets

Time-sensitive events with same-day resolution. Sports parlays. Breaking news combinations. Fed decision + market reaction in one position. Built for the news cycle, not quarterly filings.

Debate Markets New

Two traders with opposing theses go head to head. The crowd watches, reacts, and shifts odds in real time as arguments are made. The market becomes the judge. Nothing like this exists anywhere.

Leg Extraction Engine New

Won 4 of your 6-leg combo early? Strip those legs out as liquid shares and sell them independently. Your remaining 2 legs keep running. Granular exit control — not all-or-nothing.

Correlation Mining Rewards

Discover a non-obvious correlation before others — say, bond yields and a specific sports team's game-day attendance — and earn CLRV tokens as a "market maker of insight." Insight has economic value here.

Meta-Markets

"Will the BTC-$100k market hit 80% probability before Dec 1?" becomes a tradeable outcome itself. Bet on how other markets behave — not just what happens in the real world.

Fractal Markets

Create a sub-market inside your position. Bet "AI IPO in 2026" at the macro level, then let others bet on which specific company — Anthropic, OpenAI, Cohere — within your position.
